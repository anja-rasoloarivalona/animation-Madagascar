Madagascar is a huge island and a country located off the southeast coast of Africa. It is home to thousands of endemic animal species such as lemurs, as well as tropical forests, beaches and reefs. Near the bustling capital, Antananarivo, is Ambohimanga Hill, a complex of royal palaces and cemeteries, as well as Avenue des Baobabs, a dirt road lined with giant trees several centuries old.
